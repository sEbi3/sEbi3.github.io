BetterInteraction

BetterInteraction is an system used to interact with suspects, persons of interest and pedestrians. Features include ID checks, drug/alcohol testing, calling services, changeable hotkeys and settings, pedestrian transport and much more.

Installation

1.) Copy the files and folder of "Plugins\LSPDFR" in: "GTA V directory\plugins\LSPDFR".
2.) You can now go on duty!

Requirements

• LSPDFR 0.4.8 (or higher) Download here
• RageNativeUI 1.7 (Included in the download)
• RagePluginHook 1.84 (or higher) Download here
• A legal copy of GTA5 (Retail or Steam)

Current Features

• You can use the "Ped Interaction Menu", if you want to interact with the nearest Ped.
      ○ You can ask the Ped for his/her ID.
     ○ You can do a Ped Check to dispatch.
     ○ You can perform a Drug or Breathalyzer Test with the Ped.
     ○ You can ask the Ped to follow you, or to stop following you.
     ○ You can call a Transport for the Ped like an ambulance, a taxi and uber.
     ○ You can Dimiss the Ped. (Ask to leave the scene, Dismiss or give the Ped a warn)
• You can call an ambulance, a firetruck, a swat unit or an air unit with the "Emergency Services Menu".
• Call a Tow Service to pick up the nearest vehicle or a Coroner Unit to pick up dead bodies.
• You can do a Ped and Plate Check.
• You can end the current callout with the menu.
• Police Vehicle Equipment Menu
     ○ Grab a pumpshotgun, a carbinerrifle and/or fire extinguisher.
     ○ Use a medickit and/or equip Armor.
• Hotkeys for opening the menu with controller support.
     ○ You can change all current keys in the BetterInteraction.ini.
     ○ You can change all models and vehicles to your liking.
• Version checker to let you know, if there is a new version out for BetterInteraction and LSPDFR.
• Audio tone for the police radio animation. (You can replace the default audio)

Planned Features

• Developer API with functions of the menu.
• Questioning system with custom questions.
• Option to spawn a Police Partner, who is going to support you on your patrol.
• Traffic Menu (Stop traffic, slow down the traffic)
• Vehicle search with custom stuff.
